Why Lumencat?

Lumencat stands for clear, modern websites and pragmatic technology. No hype – just solutions that work, explained clearly and that make your day easier.

Focus on your day-to-day

The technology is not the centre of attention – your daily operations are. Websites and tools are planned to fit your industry, your team and your workflows. The goal: you explain, follow up and organise less.

Clean technology, clearly explained

Lumencat uses modern web standards and ready-made solutions where they make sense for your business. Many components are based on proven patterns, combined and adapted for you. You get a system that is documented, traceable and extensible.

Custom, not off-the-shelf

There is no one-size-fits-all solution. Every website and tool is planned individually. Structure, content and features are oriented around your business and your customers. The result is a presence that fits you and can grow with you.

How a project with Lumencat works

From the first message to the finished website, the process stays clear, transparent and relaxed for you. You always know the next step and what I'm taking care of.

1

Kickoff and analysis

We talk about your business, your goals and your current situation. Afterwards I summarise what you really need and which results will help you most. On this basis you get a clear recommendation and, on request, a matching quote.

2

Concept and structure

We define the structure of your website, plan the content and clarify which features you really need. Navigation, page layout and first copy ideas are developed in this phase. You see early on how everything feels and can give feedback without needing technical terms.

3

Implementation and setup

I translate the design into clean code, set up your contact form and deploy ready-made tools for your business as needed. You get to see intermediate states and give feedback before everything is finalised.

4

Launch and ongoing support

We test features, form submission and display on mobile and desktop. Then your website goes live. On request, I handle ongoing care and small adjustments to keep everything current, secure and reachable. You get a short overview of how to adjust content yourself.
